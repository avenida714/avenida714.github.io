Alec Venida

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! My name is Alec Venida, and I am software engineer in southern California.

A former ESL teacher and recent App Academy graduate, I love all things music, culture, and tech. I am humbled that you would check out my apps!

  • Check out my portfolio!

YouTut

Dedicated exclusively to online tutorials (Tuts), YouTut is my clone of YouTube and my homage to teachers online and everywhere. It is a full-stack web application featuring React/Redux on the front end, with Python and Flask in the back end. It makes use of Amazon Web Services S3 buckets so that users can upload videos directly to the site. Stream away and learn something!

  • Let's Tut!

Instacrumbz

Tired of waiting around in restaurants for people to take pictures of their food? With Instacrumbz, the camera eats last! This was a collaborative project featuring React/Redux on the front end, and Python/Flask on the backend. Special shoutout to my teammates Dawei Ting, Ray Henry, and Rudy Nguyen.

  • Sounds Yummy!

Mic-Lockr

Have a shiny new microphone you'd like to show off? Come flex your vocal cords with Mic-Lockr, my first full-stack web application (Postgres, Express, React, Node) loosely based on Flickr.
